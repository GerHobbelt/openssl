Compiling openssl 1.1.0f for Raspbian

1.Download openssl 1.1.0f from openssl website
the link: https://www.openssl.org/source/openssl-1.1.0f.tar.gz

2.Config and compile openssl for Raspbian
CROSS_COMPILE=arm-linux-gnueabihf-  ./Configure linux-armv4 no-shared no-asm zlib --prefix=target-directory
make
make install
